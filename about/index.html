About Seidecosa

We have been in the silk industry since 30 years in Bangalore & exporting silk yarn, silk fabric to the fashion industry locally & exporting to Europe, Middle East, USA & many Asian countries.

Our state Karnataka is the largest producer of silk in India & we being in this industry enjoy certain competitive advantages like right source of raw material, knowing the end to end technical know how & most importantly well versed with astonishing properties & nutrients of cocoon & silkworm pupae. To ensure optimum utilization of all the materials we expanded our firm to produce & distribute silk proteins which is an active protein ingredient used in cosmetic industry for skincare and haircare applications & also we process & supply dried silkworm pupae, silkworm pupae in powder & pellet form used as a feed ingredient for fish, shrimps & poultry feed industry and also used for human consumption.

As we are a global supplier, we adhere to all the international standards, certification requirements and specifications applicable to cosmetic & feed industry. The organization works with quality being the focal point of business. Thus, by virtue of dedication towards ensuring quality products & complete satisfaction to our customers, the products are offered as per the strict delivery schedules at competitive prices.
