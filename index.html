About Seidecosa

We have been in the silk industry since 30 years in Bangalore & exporting silk yarn to the fashion industry locally & exporting to Europe, Middle East & many more countries.

Our state Karnataka is the largest producer of silk in India & we being in this industry enjoy certain competitive advantages like right source of raw material, knowing the end to end technical know how & most importantly well versed with astonishing properties & nutrients of cocoon & silkworm pupae. To ensure optimum utilization of all the materials we expanded our firm to produce & distribute silk proteins which is an active protein ingredient used in cosmetic industry for skincare and haircare applications & also to process & supply dried silkworm pupae, silkworm pupae in powder & pellet form used as protein rich feed ingredient for fishes, shrimps & poultry farms..
