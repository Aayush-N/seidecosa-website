Silkworm Pupae Powder


As we know silkworm pupae is the by product of silk industry. The pupae are finely grinded into powder form for it to be used in feed industry as there are special request for powder form. The silkworm pupae powder is mainly used as valuable feed ingredient for poultry, fish & pet feed industry.

For export the silkworm powder stuffed in 20 ft container is only 7/9 tons.
