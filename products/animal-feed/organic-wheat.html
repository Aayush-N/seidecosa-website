Organic Wheat Meal

Wheat is not used traditionally as a feed grain because its milling properties make it desirable for use in breads, pastas and noodles. At certain times, however wheat is priced competitively with other feed grains due to damage from disease, drought or sprouting. Feed-grade wheat is a palatable, digestible source of nutrients that can be used in cattle diets if fed with caution to avoid digestive upsets.

Benefits

  1. • Significantly higher protein content and more rapid rate of starch digestion.
  2. • Excellent palatability, its consistency and low ash content make it an ingredient of choice for piglet feeds.
  3. • Very stable mineral profile is perfectly suited to poultry diets.
